My Favourite Subject

History

History isn’t just a list of dates and battles—it’s a doorway into the richness of human experience. What I enjoy most is uncovering the traditions, food, and fashion that reveal how different cultures celebrated life, mourned loss, and made sense of the world. From ancient Koreans to nomadic tribes, these stories highlight the creativity and resilience of people across time. For me, history is both inspiring and eye-opening—a subject that connects the past to the world we live in today.
